# Ikoma Sanjo Amusement Park

## Intent
An amusement park on the hill was something I was curious about, the main drive is curosity. 

## Experience
I went up via a train, the amusement park is free to visit. Payment is only for specific rides. I went to the flying plane as well as the "White House". Afterwards I climbed down the hill and walked back to the train.

## Memory
The trains are very cute and have a nice design. The flying plane is great because I was able to see both Nara and Osaka from a high overview. Additionally the White house depicted an intresting version of America, the building is tilted by a few degrees. The founding myth was shown as a bridge, then followed by a steam train. Notably the train noise is clearly a vaccum cleaner. At the end, lego was used to depict the moon landing.

## Insight
Overall the entire place was really enjoyable despite the low budget vibe. Some of the creative solutions are something to inspire me. In the future I learn how the budget does not determine the quality.
