# Wakayama
## Intent
Hot Spring are kinda what is popular in Japan, so I went to this hot spring resort. 

## Experience
As I am aware there is no food I bought some stuff before I went. It was an intresting experience as I was given something to wear in the hotel. My room was facing the ocean. I went for the hot spring and they list the composition(in japanese), soaking inside I can feel like something is on my skin. I went twice, once at night and another in the morning.

In the morning I was at the outdoors section where I can look at the nearby highway. After that I had breakfast which was a spread of random meals. I walked around Kada town a bit before departing.

## Memory
The Hot Spring certainly feel nice, it is an unique experience. It was relatively isolated since I arrive late/early respectively.

## Insight
I need to stay for more days in the future, one day isn't enough unfortuantely. 

# Reflection - Overall
All in all it was an enjoyable trip, however the last few days are hampered by poor planning making them less memorable   compared to the first few days. I hope to do this again in the future.
